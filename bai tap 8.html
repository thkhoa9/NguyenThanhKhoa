A smile is a facial expression formed primarily by flewing the muscles at the sides of the mouth. It is believed it takes about 80 muscles to smile.Some smiles include a contraction of the muscles at the corner of the eyes, an action known as a "Duchenne smile".Smiles preformed without the eye contraction may be perceived as insincere.
